About CREED

Advancing Energy Efficiency and Decarbonization

Established in 2023 at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Ropar, CREED is at the forefront of sustainable energy research.

Our mission is to be a global leader in energy research and decarbonization, fostering impactful collaborations between academia and industry to drive initiatives that promote sustainable energy practices for a greener tomorrow.
