About Us

About CREED

Advancing Energy Efficiency and Decarbonization through Innovation

Established 2023

Founded at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Ropar, CREED represents the cutting edge of sustainable energy research and innovation.

Our Mission

To be a global leader in energy research and decarbonization, fostering impactful collaborations between academia and industry to drive initiatives that promote sustainable energy practices for a greener tomorrow.

Our Services

Tailored Solutions for a Sustainable Future

Comprehensive energy solutions designed to transform industries and drive environmental impact

KISEM Project

In collaboration with IIT Madras and Kotak Mahindra Bank, we provide comprehensive, free energy audits for selected MSME industries.

Energy Audit Pro

A complete energy audit solution with detailed reporting, on-site assessments, and implementation support from our expert team.

Energy Audit Select

Focused audits for specific equipment or systems, providing targeted insights for smaller-scale energy conservation needs.

RAMP - Goa

CREED IIT Ropar is an authorised audit partner of DITC Goa. For MSME industries in Goa upto 75% audit cost can be reimbursed.

RAMP - Punjab

CREED IIT Ropar is the only audit partner of DOI and PEDA for free energy auditing of msme industries in Punjab.
