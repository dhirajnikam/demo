Our Services

Comprehensive Energy Solutions

CREED offers a suite of services designed to help organisations reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions. From large-scale audits to targeted studies, our offerings are tailored to your needs.

KISEM Programme

Free Audits

In partnership with IIT Madras and supported by CSR funding, our KISEM programme delivers comprehensive energy audits for micro-, small- and medium-sized enterprises. Selected industries receive assessments free of charge, helping them to unlock energy savings and adopt sustainable practices.

Energy Audit Pro

Comprehensive

A comprehensive audit service that combines on-site assessments with a detailed report. Our certified auditors evaluate your entire operation using advanced measurement tools and provide clear recommendations to improve efficiency and cut emissions.

Learn More

Energy Audit Select

Targeted

Tailored for clients seeking a focused solution, Energy Audit Select examines specific systems such as compressor lines or transformers. This targeted approach delivers insights on key processes without undertaking a full-site review.
