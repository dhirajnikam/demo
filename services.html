KISEM

Collaborating with IIT Madras and aided by CSR initiative by Kotak Mahindra Bank we are offering a comprehensive energy audits for MSME industries. And for selected industries we would be offering free auditing service.

Energy Audit Pro

A comprehensive energy audit solution complete with a detailed report and aid in implementing the audit suggestions.

On Site Assessment
Our team of qualified Energy auditors and engineers will visit the site and assess the entire process and industry/building. Measurements of various parameters using state-of-the-art devices.
Detailed Report
A detailed report prepared by our energy auditor and vetted by experienced professors will be submitted. The report would contain the energy conservation measures, measurement details, implementation suggestions, etc.
Support for Implementation
Our team will follow up and support the implementation of energy conservation measures.
